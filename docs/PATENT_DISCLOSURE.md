## Invention 1: Automated Insurance Lead Generation from Public Life-Event Records

### Technical Field
Systems and methods for automated generation of insurance leads by monitoring and extracting data from publicly available government records corresponding to life events that correlate with insurance purchasing intent.

### Background
The insurance lead generation industry operates as an oligopoly. A small number of lead aggregators (EverQuote, QuoteWizard, MediaAlpha) purchase consumer data and resell the same lead to 5–8 competing agents simultaneously, at prices ranging from $40 to $150 per lead. This creates a race-to-the-bottom dynamic where agents compete on price rather than service quality, and consumers receive multiple unsolicited contacts.

### Summary of Invention
The present invention provides a system that generates **exclusive** insurance leads by monitoring publicly available government records for life events that statistically correlate with insurance purchasing decisions. These records include, but are not limited to:

1. **Marriage license filings** — Newlyweds require life insurance, homeowners insurance (if purchasing together), and auto insurance policy consolidation.
2. **Real property transfer records** — Home purchases trigger homeowners, flood, umbrella, and mortgage protection insurance needs.
3. **Business entity formation filings** — New business registrations create demand for commercial general liability, workers' compensation, E&O, and key-man life insurance.
4. **Birth and adoption records** — New parents require life insurance, health insurance riders, and education savings products.
5. **Vehicle registration records** — New vehicle registrations, particularly for high-risk drivers, trigger SR-22 filing requirements.
6. **Divorce filings** — Divorcing parties require policy separation, beneficiary updates, and new individual coverage.

### Novel Aspects
The invention is novel in the following respects:

**A. Automated Multi-Source Aggregation with AI Extraction**  
The system employs a Playwright-based web automation framework to systematically retrieve public records from county clerk websites, Secretary of State portals, and recorder of deeds databases. An AI language model then extracts structured lead data (name, contact information, event type, date, jurisdiction) from unstructured HTML and PDF documents.

**B. Life-Event-to-Insurance-Vertical Mapping**  
The system automatically maps each life event type to one or more insurance verticals, enabling targeted outreach. A marriage license triggers simultaneous lead creation for life insurance, homeowners, and auto agents. A business filing triggers commercial lines outreach.

**C. Exclusive Lead Model**  
Unlike aggregator-sold leads, leads generated by this system are exclusive to the subscribing agent. The system tracks lead assignment and prevents duplicate outreach from multiple agents.

**D. Integrated Lead Scoring and Prioritization**  
The system scores leads based on recency of the life event, geographic proximity to the agent, and historical conversion rates for similar lead profiles.

### Claims (Provisional)
1. A computer-implemented method for generating insurance leads comprising: monitoring publicly available government records for life events; extracting contact information associated with said life events using machine learning; mapping said life events to insurance product categories; and presenting said leads exclusively to subscribing insurance agents.

2. The method of claim 1, wherein said life events include at least one of: marriage license issuance, real property transfer, business entity formation, birth record filing, and vehicle registration.

3. A system comprising: a web automation module configured to retrieve public records from government websites; a natural language processing module configured to extract structured data from said records; a lead management database; and an agent interface for exclusive lead distribution.

## Invention 2: AI-Powered 24/7 Insurance Phone Agent Using FOSS Components

### Technical Field
Systems and methods for providing automated telephone-based insurance consultation services using a combination of open-source speech recognition, neural text-to-speech synthesis, and large language model reasoning.

### Summary of Invention
The present invention provides a fully automated telephone answering system that functions as a licensed insurance agent's virtual receptionist, capable of:

1. Answering inbound calls 24 hours per day, 7 days per week
2. Identifying the caller's insurance need through natural conversation
3. Routing callers to the appropriate insurance vertical
4. Providing accurate policy information, pricing estimates, and state compliance requirements
5. Booking consultation appointments with human agents
6. Generating complete call transcripts and AI summaries for agent review

### Novel Aspects

**A. FOSS-First Architecture**  
The system is implemented entirely using open-source components: OpenAI Whisper (STT), Piper TTS (voice synthesis), and Vocode (orchestration), achieving parity with commercial systems (Nuance, Google CCAI) at zero per-call cost.

**B. Insurance Domain Knowledge Integration**  
The underlying language model is augmented with a proprietary knowledge base covering SR-22/FR-44 filing requirements for all 50 states, carrier pricing data, coverage type definitions, and state-specific compliance requirements.

**C. Vertical Detection and Routing**  
The system employs intent classification to identify which of the supported insurance verticals (SR-22, burial, pet, tiny home, gig economy) the caller requires, and adjusts its conversational flow accordingly.

**D. Lead Capture Integration**  
All caller information collected during the call is automatically entered into the lead management system, with the detected vertical and call outcome recorded for agent follow-up.

## Invention 3: Insurance Agent Wellness and Motivation Platform

### Technical Field
Systems and methods for reducing occupational burnout among insurance agents through AI-personalized motivational content, goal tracking, and wellness monitoring.

### Background
Insurance agents experience one of the highest burnout rates in professional services. The combination of commission-only compensation, high rejection rates, and complex regulatory compliance creates significant psychological stress. Agent attrition rates in the industry exceed 80% within the first year.

### Summary of Invention
The present invention provides a platform integrated within an insurance agent's workflow tools that:

1. Conducts daily mood check-ins using a structured assessment
2. Generates AI-personalized affirmations calibrated to the agent's current emotional state and recent performance data
3. Tracks commission goals with visual progress indicators
4. Analyzes mood trends over time to identify burnout risk
5. Provides curated motivational content specific to insurance industry challenges

### Novel Aspects

**A. Context-Aware Affirmation Generation**  
Unlike generic motivational apps, the system generates affirmations that reference the specific challenges of insurance sales (rejection handling, SR-22 stigma, cold calling anxiety) and incorporates the agent's actual performance data.

**B. Burnout Early Warning System**  
The system analyzes patterns in daily check-in data to identify agents at risk of burnout before attrition occurs, enabling proactive intervention.
